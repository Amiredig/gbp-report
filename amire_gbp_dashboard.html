Key Observations & Recommendations

📈 Strong Q1 2025 Peak — Mar Best Month
March 2025 was the strongest month on record with 185 total views and 40 interactions (21.6% action rate). Direction requests hit 35 in March and 44 in April, indicating strong local purchase intent. A seasonal content push in Feb–Mar each year may amplify this pattern.
📉 Post-Sep 2025 Interaction Collapse
Despite views holding at 100–160/month, customer interactions dropped sharply from October 2025 — falling from 21 in September to just 3–7/month through 2026. Direction requests dropped to zero from June 2025 onwards. Reviewing the profile for CTA completeness and category accuracy is a priority action.
🖥️ Desktop Search Dominates at 44.2%
Desktop accounts for 988 of 1,571 search views (62.9%), which is unusually high for a local business. This likely reflects B2B or research-intent discovery rather than on-the-go local lookups — desktop users probably expect more detailed service information when they land on the website.
🔍 Non-Brand GBP Keywords Near Zero
Only "amire" generated measurable impressions (18 total). Local terms like "seo agency northern beaches" and "digital marketing manly" appear in the keyword list but are not surfacing the GBP listing. Adding keyword-rich GBP posts, Q&As, and service descriptions would directly address this gap.
